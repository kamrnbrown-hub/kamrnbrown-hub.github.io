Birth Chart / Natal Chart
A snapshot of exactly where every planet sat in the sky the moment you were born. It's fixed forever — it never changes, no matter how much time passes.
Transit
Where a planet is right now, compared to where it was in your birth chart. When today's moon lines up near where a planet sat when you were born, that planet is "being transited" — it's briefly activated again.
Rising Sign / Ascendant
The zodiac sign that was rising on the horizon the moment you were born. It's essentially your outward personality — first impressions, how you move through the world — separate from your inner Sun sign self.
Gate (Human Design)
One of 64 specific energies mapped from the I Ching. Each planet in your chart "activates" a gate based on exactly where it sat when you were born — that gate becomes a lifelong theme in how you express yourself.
Channel
When both gates on either end of a connection are activated, they form a Channel — a strong, consistent flow of energy between two Centers in your body graph.
Defined vs. Open Center
A defined (colored-in) center is energy you have consistent, reliable access to. An open (white) center is where you're more absorbent — you take in and amplify energy from other people and your environment.
Incarnation Cross
Built from your Sun and Earth gates (both Design and Personality) — this is considered your overarching life purpose or theme in Human Design.
