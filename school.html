Your Real Estate Investment Connection
▾
Property underwriting knowledge teaches you to see risk the way smart investors do — construction quality, location hazards, prior losses, flood zones, building age.
NYC's complex insurance needs (co-ops, condos, brownstones, mixed-use) make understanding insurance both a career asset and an investing edge.
After 3–5 years, Chubb, PCS, and Misch Group specialize in insuring luxury NYC real estate — this is where underwriting and real estate investing overlap most.
How to Stand Out When Applying
▾
• Get your P&C license BEFORE applying — it immediately separates you from unlicensed candidates
• Mention your real estate investment interest in interviews — shows you understand property risk intuitively
• Reference GNY's trainee program specifically — shows you did your homework
• Study the CPCU designation path — mentioning it shows long-term commitment
• Connect with underwriters on LinkedIn at target companies before applying
